SUPERVISION OF KINDERGARTENS

    However, for quite some time now, the demand for places in kindergartens for pre-school age children has been very great. Recently, there has been a spate of finance companies advertising for deposits from the public offering to pay rather high rates of interest.     22. "No allocation of land in the Malay settlement at Ayer Gemuroh has been made since 1959. The Government, taking cognizance of the acute shortage of land, has decided that the policy regarding such settlement should be reviewed in the light of our rapidly increasing population.

Column: 524

time of gazetting in March 1959 there were already 190 Malay, 16 Chinese and 5 Indian families living on it. In the same year, 8 new families moved into the area and were allocated one lot each.
