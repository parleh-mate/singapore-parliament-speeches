1980 second tier NWC payment will not be paid in each subsequent year in the way it is implemented in the private sector, and, if so, why the Government, as an employer, appears to have departed from its original position when it accepted the 1980 NWC recommendations in toto.

     The Minister for Finance (Mr Hon Sui Sen): Mr Deputy Speaker, Sir, the Member for Moulmein is right that Government will be paying the second tier NWC wage increase for 1980 as a one-time lump sum to deserving civil servants.
