    7. Mr Harun A. Ghani asked the Minister for Manpower if he will give an update on the employment situation since January 1999 to date, in particular, (a) how many new jobs were created; (b) how many jobs were lost; and (c) whether workers can expect to see better job prospects for the rest of 1999.
