     Mr Teh Cheang Wan: Mr Speaker, Sir, there are several reasons for HDB exercising its powers. Another reason is non-occupation of an HDB flat, in other words, a person has bought the flat but never occupied the flat, and has left the flat vacant. There are also cases of making false declaration in the application; a misuse of premises, such as using the flat as a brothel. Then there are cases of ownership of private property. Under the HDB rules, HDB flat owners are not allowed to own private residential property.
