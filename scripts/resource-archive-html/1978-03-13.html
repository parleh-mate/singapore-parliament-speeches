     Mr Ng Kah Ting (Punggol): Mr Speaker, Sir, could the Hon. Minister describe the types of apprenticeship schemes?     Under the scheme, employers are encouraged to recruit apprentices well in advance in anticipation of projected manpower needs and to sponsor them through a period of full-time institutional training prior to on-the-job training. After the period of institutional training, apprentices are normally put through a two-year period of on-the-job training on the shop floor.
