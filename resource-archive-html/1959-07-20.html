I would like to say that the Singapore police has served conscientiously and this can be proved. To say that the police is an instrument of Colonial rulers is nonsensical and truly misleading. The services of the police in Singapore have been made at great sacrifice as can be seen in the history of Singapore from the time that it inculcated the spirit of anti-Colonialism until today. If the Singapore police is considered an instrument or a thing apart from the people, and not fulfilling its duties, then I am sure that Singapore will not be what it is today. Who has been responsible for bringing about the peace which has resulted in Singapore being what it is today? The Singapore police has taken an active part in clearing Singapore of Communist subversion, whether from within or without. Therefore, Mr Speaker, I cannot agree that the Singapore police is an instrument of Colonial rule. If the Singapore police was
