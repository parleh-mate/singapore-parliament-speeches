    2. Mr David Marshall asked the Minister for Finance what is (a) the actual revenue for 1960 compared with that for 1959; and (b) the estimated revenue for 1962 compared with that for 1961.

    The Parliamentary Secretary to the Minister for Finance (Mr S. Ramaswamy) (for the Minister for Finance): Mr Speaker, Sir, I have been asked to reply. The actual revenue for the year 1960 was $311,264,201 compared with $274,918,527 for 1959, that is, an increase of about $36.3 million.

    The 1962 revenue estimate is $391,109,000 compared with the 1961 estimate of $344,808,610, that is, an increase of roughly $46.3 million.
