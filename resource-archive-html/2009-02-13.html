    First of all, in respect of creating a vibrant legal services sector.  Last December, we granted Qualifying Foreign Law Practice (QFLP) licences to six foreign law firms.  These six firms are top-notch and internationally renowned.  They will complement and enhance the legal services provided in Singapore.
