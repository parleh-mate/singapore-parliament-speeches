But, their promotion prospects are slim, and they get increments of only $20 to $30. Gradually, the teaching career is no longer regarded as a career with a promising future.

Moreover, some teachers are merely made to act as Heads of Department without official appointment by the Ministry of Education. As a result, many capable teachers do not want to be made Heads of Department. They feel that the Ministry of Education is just exploiting them, with no real intention of promoting them.
