    This Bill proposes amendments to the Singapore Legislative Assembly Elections Ordinance which are rendered necessary by the enactment of the Singapore Citizenship Ordinance, 1957.     Singapore is to attain the status of the State of Singapore as a self-governing territory, and the Assembly will be a fully elected body when the Legislative Assembly elections under the new Constitution are held. Singapore Citizenship Ordinance, a charter by which we have achieved what we longed for years - the creation of a status recognisable in all parts of the Commonwealth for all those who regard Singapore as their home and the object of their undivided loyalty.

    Those who are citizens by birth and descent have the right to vote. The proposed amendment to section 5 is very significant. Thousands of our adult population who, although not born in Singapore, have been resident here for long periods and have contributed to the prosperity of Singapore, and are enthusiastically becoming Singapore citizens and declaring their allegiance to Singapore. It is important that the new status of Singapore citizenship should carry with it the right to assist in the procedure of democratic government, and this Bill seeks to grant our new citizens their first democratic right-the right to vote for election of the people's representatives to sit in this House.

    The general purpose of this Bill, therefore, is to provide that the right to vote in the Legislative Assembly elections shall be granted to citizens of Singapore and to Singapore citizens only, in a self-governing Singapore, it is no longer desirable to offer voting rights to those whose bodies are in Singapore but whose hearts and minds are elsewhere. If the newly won Constitution for self-government is to be implemented in the spirit in which it was framed, it is essential that the hearts and minds of all people participating in the government of Singapore should be in Singapore itself, and that their outlook and undivided loyalty should be centred here. Therefore, voting rights should be accorded only to citizens of Singapore.
