WALK-WAYS AND PEDESTRIAN-MALLS


    3. Mr J.B. Jeyaretnam asked the Minister for National Development if he will give the total amount spent on the construction, reconditioning, renovation and renewal of walk-ways and pedestrian-malls in 1982.

     Mr Teh Cheang Wan: In 1982, a total of $502,002 was spent on the construction of new pedestrian walkways and footpaths. Another $2,309,539 was spent on the repairs and maintenance of existing footpaths and kerbs.
