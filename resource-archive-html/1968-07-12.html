IMPROVEMENT OF DRAINAGE SYSTEM IN JOO CHIAT


    1. Mr Yeoh Ghim Seng asked the Minister for Law and National Development whether he is aware that the drainage system in the Joo Chiat Constituency is inadequate and whether and what steps he proposes to take to improve it.

of rubbish into the drains by some residents of the area. The lands in this area are generally low-lying and the drainage system rather antiquated.
