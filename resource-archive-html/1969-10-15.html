INCOME TAX RELIEFS
FOR AGED PARENTS


    1. Mr Ng Kah Ting asked the Minister for Finance if he will consider introducing amending legislation to allow income tax reliefs to tax-payers who are maintaining their aged parents.

    The Minister for Finance (Dr Goh Keng Swee): Mr Speaker, Sir, in Asian societies, the support of aged parents is an acknowledged responsibility of all decent citizens. In these circumstances, it would be unworthy of us to introduce mercenary considerations such as relief from income tax.

FREE PRIMARY EDUCATION FOR CHILDREN OF NON-CITIZEN

    4. Mr Ng Kah Ting asked the Minister for Education whether free primary education will be given to children born in the Republic of parents who are non-citizens but are permanent residents.
