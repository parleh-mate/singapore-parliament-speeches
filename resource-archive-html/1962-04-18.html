    "Mr David Marshall: To ask the Prime Minister what is the area occupied by the Royal Singapore Golf Club and the terms on which such lands are held.".

    "The Royal Singapore Golf Club occupies an area of approximately 270 acres out of a total of 7,573 acres of City Council catchment land. This is covered by two separate Agreements:-
