    "That, in pursuance of the national objective of a united Malayan nation, enlarged and strengthened by the addition of our brothers in the Borneo territories, this House declares its support in principle for the plan proposed by the Prime Minister of the Federation of Malaya, Tunku Abdul Rahman Putra Al-Haj, and agreed to in principle in the Federation Parliament on 18th October, 1961, for the establishment of the Federation of Malaysia, comprising the eleven States of the present Federation, the States of Singapore and Brunei, and the territories of Sarawak and North Borneo.".

    In line 4, to leave out "plan" and insert "motion";

In a write-up in May in Hong Lim, and in June before Anson, the Communist front organisations in the unions and the most important Communist front leader, Mr Lim Ching Siong, were inviting us, the P.A.P., to take the lead in the anti-colonial struggle.
