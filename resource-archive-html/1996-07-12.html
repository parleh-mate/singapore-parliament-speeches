     The Minister for Labour (Dr Lee Boon Yang): Mr Speaker, Sir, the interest paid by the CPF Board for savings in the Ordinary and Medisave Accounts is computed on a monthly basis and the rate is pegged to the average of the savings and 12-month fixed deposit rates of the Big Four local banks.
