(Integration)


    1. Mr R. Sinnakaruppan asked the Minister for Communications if the proposed Electronic Road Pricing gadgets to be fitted into motor vehicles will be integrated with the proposed electronic meters for car parking.

     The Minister of State for Communications (Dr John Chen Seow Phun) (for the Minister for Communications): Mr Speaker, Sir, we are the first country to introduce ERP on such a large scale.
