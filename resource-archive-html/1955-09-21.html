We naturally had our trade relations in mind, for in seeking our basic aim to improve the standard of living of the people of this territory, we must be ever watchful to improve the general prosperity of the island. The inspiration for the goodwill mission, however, Sir, went well beyond the question of trade.

    If the principal objective of the people of this territory - independence - is to have any real meaning, we must now seek to lay the foundations of friendly relations which will transform our immediate neighbours into our allies of tomorrow.
