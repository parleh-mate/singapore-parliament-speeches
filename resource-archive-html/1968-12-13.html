Column: 212



    Mr Tang See Chim (for the Minister for Finance): Mr Speaker, Sir, the licensing of new factories to manufacture products on the Control of Manufacture list where these products are already being produced by existing factories would depend on the volume and capacity production of such factories as the local market could bear. The Government is prepared to consider the issue of new licences if it is established that the production of existing manufacturers is not sufficient to cater for the needs and requirements of local consumers. However, the Government would like to satisfy itself that new manufacturers have the necessary managerial, technical and marketing expertise required for successful operation of their enterprise. It is, however, difficult to enforce compliance with undertakings given in respect of export performance. Accordingly, such undertakings are treated with reserve.
